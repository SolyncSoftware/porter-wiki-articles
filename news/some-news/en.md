## Hello, it's papertek

Let's talk about what's been happening recently.

## The bad news

After a full year of partnership, it has ended. We're no longer involved with Netro Corporation. A company can't claim to be a worker-owned collective while being run unilaterally.

## The good news

Theaceae Collective is still ours; we will begin to move forward independently under a new name, too.

We are calling it **Solync** (pronounced "so-link"), and we're already building some cool shit. Our values remain: a flat, collaborative environment with no centralized power.

If you were part of our journey and want to join Solync in this new chapter, [visit our website](https://solync.org/)! If not, we wish you well...

Also, after many tests, the doctors found out I don't have cancer! Wahoo!!

## What happens to Theaceae Collective websites?

We still control them. We'll also temporarily move them to [UNTONE](https://untone.org/)'s infrastructure! We plan to host the websites on our own infrastructure eventually, so stay tuned. :D

Anyway, I'm very tired, and I need rest.

If you have any questions, feedback, issues, or wish to help out, you can add me on Discord <mark>@papertek</mark>, or you can join the community [Discord](https://discord.gg/nUeRyRtDYC).

Thank you for reading! :)
